KLC Holdco, LLC

The IPO of Nextdoor, a company preliminary valued at $2.20B, is much expected by many investors. Founded in 2008 and headquartered in San Francisco, CA, the company launched its first product in 2011, which is now available in 11 countries, including France and Germany. The product is actually a social network, where the posts are visible only to the users located nearby. Thus, its main competition is Facebook, which is also introducing additional features for the users based in the same location.

Although Nextdoor is an indirect competitor to Facebook, it is a part of the Facebook Audience Network, which means that Facebook users can post their ads on Nextdoor.
